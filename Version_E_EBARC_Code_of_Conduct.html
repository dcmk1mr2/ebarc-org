EBARC Code of Conduct


Members will support a friendly, supportive, and collaborative volunteer environment that will reflect positively upon the East Bay Amateur Radio Club, its members, and amateur radio generally. Members will act with integrity and honesty, and will properly use Club property. Members will be trustworthy and respectful, keep their commitments, and be accountable for their own actions. Members will refrain from harassing, bullying, intimidating, demeaning, humiliating, embarrassing, or attacking another person visually, verbally, or physically.
